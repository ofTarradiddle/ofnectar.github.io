Still Evolving

We love food stuffs

We have essentially used a search algorithm guided by tastes, and chemical secretions, that during evolution have guided us towards proper foods. This, perhaps, is a survivor bias, with the observation that we have certain traits today having survived. Ancestral neighbors may have died away from improper consumption habits, and thus we are healthier following certain patterns than others. Historically bad traits would die with its host, but with modern medicine and new technology, persons with chronic diseases can survive.
Previously we have been curtailed by surviving to decide what we are to become, we are now in a position to make our own choices in what we shall grow into, and we have seen the obesity, addictions, and other chronic diseases manifest. As a whole, we are choosing to become the wrong things. This is partly due to a lack of available resources to understand diseases, and prevention, but also mis information. We, having survived so far, have a certain aptitude towards adaptation, through curated information & research, of Nectar aims to allow the directed evolution of the individual, and society.

This contribution will be in the form of a cultural innovations in foods that promote health conscious ideals, directed applications to improvement, while also attracting their use.
